Frontend

  • Simple: Call directly the Backend Services to get Customers and Products
  • Eureka: Call the Backend Services via Eureka Service Discovery to get Customers and Products
  • Eureka+Ribbon : Call the Backend Services via Eureka Service Discovery and Ribbon Load Balancing to get Customers and Products
  • Hystrix: Call the Backend Services with Hystrix Support to get Customers and Products

  • Zuul: Route to jsonplaceholder.typicode.com using Zuul to get Posts
